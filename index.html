About me

I am a 4th year Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley. I am working in Prof. Tomizuka's Mechanical Systems Control (MSC) Lab.

My research interests include nonlinear control, dynamical system modeling, optimal control, machine learning, and their applications on robotics.
